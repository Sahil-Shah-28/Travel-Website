Maharashtra, a state rich in history and natural beauty, offers a diverse range of experiences. Explore the ancient rock-cut caves of Ajanta and Ellora, adorned with intricate sculptures and paintings. Visit the serene hill stations of Mahabaleshwar and Panchgani, where lush greenery and valley views await. Discover the modern hill city of Lavasa, inspired by Italy’s Portofino, and immerse yourself in the spiritual vibes of Shirdi, home to the revered Sai Baba.

Located in western India, Gujarat is a vibrant state brimming with culture, history, and natural beauty. From the bustling city of Ahmedabad to the historic sites of Vadodara, there’s no shortage of fascinating places to explore. Take a safari in Gir National Park to spot the majestic Asiatic lion, marvel at the intricate carvings of the Rani Ki Vav stepwell in Patan, or visit the famous white desert of Kutch. Indulge in delicious vegetarian cuisine and witness colorful local festivals such as Navratri and the International Kite Festival.

Goa, located on India’s western coast, offers a delightful blend of natural beauty, history, and vibrant culture. Explore the Basilica de Bom Jesus, a UNESCO World Heritage Site adorned with stunning Baroque architecture. Perched atop a hill, the Chapora Fort provides panoramic views of the coastline. Don’t miss the iconic Church of Our Lady of the Immaculate Conception in Panaji. For nature enthusiasts, the majestic Dudhsagar Falls and the serene Salim Ali Bird Sanctuary await. And if you’re seeking local experiences, visit the bustling Anjuna Flea Market for handicrafts and immerse yourself in the sensory delights of Spice Farms. Goa’s charm lies not only in its beaches but also in its rich heritage
